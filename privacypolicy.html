1 Who We Are

Painting Now ("we", "our", "us") operates the website at paintingnow.info and associated landing pages, including this roof restoration quote service. We are based in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia and operate in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal inforamtion collected through our quote request forms and website.

2 inforamtion We Collect

We collect personal inforamtion you voluntarily provide when submitting a quote request, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Postcode / suburb
  • Project timeline preference

We also automatically collect certain technical inforamtion when you visit our site, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URL, via standard server logs and analytics tools.

3 How We Use Your inforamtion

We use your personal inforamtion to:

  • Connect you with a local Melbourne roof restoration partner who will provide your free quote
  • Send you relevant inforamtion about our services, where you have consented
  • Improve our website, landing pages, and advertising campaigns
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve any disputes
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity

4 How We Share Your inforamtion

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it only in these limited circumstances:

  • Local trade partners: Licensed Melbourne roof restoration contractors who will contact you with your requested quote.
  • Service providers: Third-party platforms (e.g. Make/Integromat for webhook delivery, email platforms) that process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements.
  • Analytics & advertising: Aggregated or pseudonymised data shared with Meta Platforms (Facebook Pixel) and Google Analytics to measure campaign performance. This data does not directly identify you.
  • Legal requirements: Where required by law, court order, or government authority.
Partner disclosure: When a local roofing partner contacts you, they become an independent data controller for that interaction and will handle your inforamtion under their own privacy policy.

5 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses the following tracking technologies:

  • Meta Pixel: Tracks page views and lead conversions to measure the effectiveness of Facebook/Instagram advertising. See Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics (if applicable): Measures website traffic and user behaviour in aggregated, anonymised form.
  • Functional cookies: Session cookies that support form functionality and do not track you across other sites.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit certain website features.

6 Data Storage & Security

Your data is transmitted securely via HTTPS and stored on servers located in Australia and/or the European Union (via Make/Integromat). We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your inforamtion against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

We retain your personal inforamtion for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law — typically no longer than 3 years from the date of your enquiry.

7 Your Rights & Choices

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal inforamtion we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or out-of-date inforamtion
  • Request deletion of your personal inforamtion (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian inforamtion Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 11 below.
